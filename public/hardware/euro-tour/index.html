Euro Tour

Your pedalboard, in eurorack.

Bidirectional audio level translator between 1/4" instrument/pedal level and 3.5mm eurorack level. Two independent unidirectional channels in one pedal-format box.

What It Does

Channel 1: 1/4" IN → 3.5mm OUT
Gain pot amplifies pedal/instrument up to euro level

Channel 2: 3.5mm IN → 1/4" OUT
Attenuator pot brings euro down to pedal level

Why It Exists

Pedalboards and eurorack speak different audio languages. Euro Tour translates both directions in one box, no compromise.

  • Plug a guitar straight into a modular FX chain
  • Send a VCO out through your favorite pedalboard
  • Both at once

Specifications

  • Format: 1590BB pedal enclosure
  • Power: 9V center-negative (standard pedalboard supply)
  • Circuit: TL072-based
  • Controls: 2 pots (gain + attenuator)
  • I/O: 4 jacks (2× 1/4", 2× 3.5mm)
