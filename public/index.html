What We Do

Circuit-Accurate Modeling

Most digital audio approximates analog behavior. We simulate it. Our Wave Digital Filter engine models actual component physics — Kirchhoff's laws, energy relationships, nonlinear dynamics — in real time.

Seattle Craftsmanship

Our hardware is machined and assembled in-house at our Georgetown workshop. Our software is developed by engineers who understand both code and solder. Craftsman-quality tools built by people who use them.

Open Foundation

Every product is built on the open-source PedalKernel engine. The approach is transparent, the math is rigorous, and the community validates the science. When you buy Puget Audio, you support open audio development.
