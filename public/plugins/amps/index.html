Why It’s Different

It cleans up like a real amp.

Roll back the guitar volume knob and it transitions from crunch to clean naturally. The single most cited complaint about every other amp sim. Solved.

It sags like a real amp.

Hit a heavy chord and the power stage compresses as the virtual tubes draw current. This is the “feel” guitarists mean when they say a real amp “breathes.” It emerges from the physics.

It can be modded like a real amp.

Flip the amp around. Swap tube types. Adjust bias. Snip the bright cap. Real mods, real-time. No other amp sim can do this because no other amp sim models at this level.
