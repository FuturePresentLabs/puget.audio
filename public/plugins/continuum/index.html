How It Works

Every component in the signal path is modeled as a Wave Digital Filter element. When you turn the Rate knob on DRIFT, you’re changing a capacitor value in a simulated RC network. When MONSOON’s repeats darken, it’s because the per-stage high-frequency rolloff of a 4096-stage BBD is being computed in real time.

This is circuit-accurate, not sampled. The nonlinear behavior, the clock feedthrough artifacts, the companding distortion of real bucket-brigade devices—it’s all there because the physics requires it.

Patent-Pending BBD Modeling

Three pedals in the Continuum—DRIFT, MONSOON, and NEBULA—use our proprietary bucket-brigade device modeling solution. We’ve filed a provisional patent on this approach. It captures clock feedthrough, companding artifacts, and per-stage frequency rolloff that no other plugin replicates.
