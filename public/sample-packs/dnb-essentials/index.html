DnB Essentials Vol. 1

A comprehensive drum & bass sample pack built from the ground up in our Seattle studio. Every sound recorded through real analog signal chains — no recycled loops, no generic presets.

What’s Inside

Drum Breaks & One-Shots

Tight, punchy breaks and individual hits designed for modern DnB production:

  • Kick Drums — 40+ kicks from deep subs to punchy mid-range
  • Snares & Claps — 60+ snares, rim shots, and layered claps
  • Hi-Hats — 50+ hats, rides, and metallic textures
  • Full Breaks — 30+ processed breakbeat loops at 170-178 BPM
  • Percussion — 40+ shakers, tambourines, and ghost hits

Bass

Sub-frequency weight and mid-range aggression:

  • Sub Bass — 30+ clean sine and shaped sub hits
  • Reese Bass — 40+ detuned, filtered, and distorted reese textures
  • Neuro Bass — 25+ mangled, FM-processed bass sounds
  • Bass Loops — 20+ rhythmic bass patterns at 170-178 BPM

Synths & Textures

Atmospheric elements to fill the stereo field:

  • Pads — 25+ evolving atmospheric pads
  • Stabs — 20+ chord stabs and hits
  • Leads — 15+ melodic lead sounds
  • Textures — 30+ risers, sweeps, and noise layers

FX & Atmospherics

  • Risers & Drops — 25+ tension builders and impacts
  • Foley — 20+ organic textures and found sounds
  • Vocal Chops — 15+ processed vocal hits and phrases
  • Transitions — 20+ fills, rolls, and glitch effects

Signal Chain

Every sample passes through real hardware:

  • Recorded through: Universal Audio Apollo x8p
  • Processed with: SSL Bus Compressor, Neve 1073 preamps
  • Mastered at: 24-bit / 48kHz
  • Dithered to: 24-bit for final delivery
