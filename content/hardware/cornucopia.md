# Cornucopia Eurorack Case

A Eurorack case system designed for musicians who want flexibility without compromise. Built from premium aluminum with a modular width system that grows with your setup.

## Base Configuration

**Starting at $449**

### Width Options

Select the perfect size for your rig:

- **84HP** — $449
  - Compact, portable
  - Fits in a backpack
  - Perfect for small systems or sub-mixers

- **104HP** — $549  
  - The sweet spot
  - Room for a complete voice
  - Most popular choice

- **126HP** — $649
  - Maximum flexibility
  - Full system capacity
  - Studio centerpiece

### Standard Features (All Sizes)

- **Construction:** 3mm aluminum panels, steel rails
- **Depth:** 65mm internal (fits 99% of modules)
- **Rails:** Black anodized aluminum, threaded M3
- **Bus Board:** 16-pin headers, keyed for safety
- **Power:** Mounting for Intellijel TPS80W (sold separately)
- **Ventilation:** Passive cooling, no fans needed
- **Feet:** Non-slip rubber or optional rack ears
