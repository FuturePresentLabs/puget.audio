# AcidBath

**TB-303 Voice**

A complete TB-303-style voice in Eurorack. WDF-modeled diode-ladder filter, accent circuit, slide circuit, and square/saw oscillator — all the squelch, none of the eBay tax.

## What It Does

- TB-303 voice path: VCO → diode ladder VCF → VCA
- Accent + slide circuits modeled at component level
- CV in for pitch (V/oct), gate, accent, slide
- Audio out at eurorack level

## Why It Exists

303s sound the way they sound because of how the filter, accent, and envelope interact. AcidBath models that interaction circuit-accurate so it squelches like the real thing — and lives in your rack instead of on your desk.
