# Roadie

**Your expression pedal, in eurorack.**

Converts a standard TRS expression pedal into eurorack-compatible CV. Plug in any expression pedal, get 0–~4V CV out, scaled by the RANGE knob.

## What It Does

- Accepts standard TRS expression pedal input
- Outputs 0–~4V CV (eurorack standard)
- RANGE knob scales the output voltage
- Your foot now controls filter cutoffs, VCAs, anything CV-controllable

## Why It Exists

Pedal players modulate things with their feet. Modular doesn't speak expression pedal natively. Roadie bridges the gap — your foot now controls anything CV-controllable in a rack.

## Specifications

- **Format:** 1590B pedal enclosure
- **Power:** 9V center-negative
- **Circuit:** TL072 dual-buffered
- **Input:** TRS expression pedal
- **Output:** 3.5mm CV (0–~4V)
- **Control:** RANGE knob
