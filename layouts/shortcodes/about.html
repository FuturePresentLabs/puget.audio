What We Do

Simulate Analog

Our plugins model actual component physics — resistors, capacitors, tubes, transformers — in real time, on your laptop. When simulation gets you closer to the circuit than a capture or a convolution, we simulate.

Sample Analog

Some sounds only exist on hardware. Our sample packs are recorded through real analog gear in our Seattle studio — Moog DFAM, preamps, tape — captured at 24-bit / 48 kHz with the signal chain printed on the box.

Without the 2026 Tax

Vintage iron is expensive, scarce, and getting worse. Our goal is to hand you the sound — the harmonic saturation, the dynamics, the weirdness — without the rack, the reshell, or the insurance rider. Built on the open-source PedalKernel engine.
